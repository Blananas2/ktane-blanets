On the Subject of Venus

What to do in situations of great stress: breathe in, die.

If there is a standard module background, or the planet shown is not Venus, you are looking at the wrong module.

Pressing the sphere near the status light will make the planet disappear, until said sphere is pressed again.

Pressing any of the spheres on Venus' surface will activate the module.

Once activated, the spheres on the the surface of Dark Venus will change color. To solve the module, reorient Dark Venus in such a way that the four colors at the sides of Dark Venus match those of the spheres. These spheres will now move the color on the front of the module in that direction.

Use the diagram below to determine which moves are valid. Valid moves are those where you can move to an adjacent state with a sphere press. Making an invalid move results in a strike.
